A native of Costa Rica, I moved to the U.S.A. to pursue my dream of becoming a professional percussionist. I have been playing percussion for the past 25 years now and was lucky to attend the best conservatories and schools in the nation. I am a proud alumni of the Interlochen Arts Academy, Oberlin Conservatory of Music, Boston University and Lynn Conservatory. I have performed side by side with members of the best orchestras in the world ranging from the Chicago Symphony to the Berlin Philharmonic. I am an endorsed artist from the Avedis Zildjian Family as well as the Remo Inc. Family. As a teacher and facilitator, I have been bringing the drum workshop idea to retirement communities, after school music programs and churches.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can anybody do it?

    • Absolutely! Participants range from ages 5-90 years old. The main focus is to have fun while drumming and taking advantage of the vast benefits of playing music! No experience is needed.

  • Do I need my own instrument?

    • You are welcome to bring your own instrument but my fee will provide enough instruments for everybody to play!.

  • How much do you charge?

    • Let's connect to find out! My fees are related to the group size and time.
